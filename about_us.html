About Us

Who We Are

Acrohouse is a business that provides two lodgings in the center of Athens. I, Giannis, inspired by love and solidarity towards my fellow human beings, got into the sector of hospitality. Driven by the will to provide and offer, our company provides high level hospitality and services to people from all over the world. Our spaces are perfect for the most beautiful moments of your lives and we guarantee that your stay will be filled with unique and unforgettable moments. The luxurious rooms and fully equipped areas of the houses will give you many moments of relaxation. Sharing this love and passion for service combined with amenities that the lodgings provide, it would be our honour to meet you and host you!!!
